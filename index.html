Manaty Research Center

Manaty research center is the division of Manaty dedicated to studies and research in science. It is aimed to do science in a way that benefit all and allow everyone to get involved.

  • Anyone can join the scientific commitee and participate to its decisions.
  • Researchers commit to work in an open way and to report and discuss regularily on their studies and researches.
  • Every publication or production is copy left and open source

Janus project

Janus project, led by Jean-Pierre Petit is a cosmological model that is an extension of the general relativity model providing explanation to several cosmological challenges without involving black matter or energy.
