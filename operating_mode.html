Research center operating mode

Manaty Research Center allows Researchers to participate in Research Projects and get financed by the Scientific commitee.

Researchers

Researchers are individuals that are working, full or part time, on one or more Research Projects.
They work openly by committing their work on public git repositories or drives.
They regularily participate to webinars to present their studies and research.

To become a Manaty Researcher, send a request on discord.

Projects

Projects are entities:

  • Created and led by a project leader.
  • Constituted of Researchers.
  • Financed and supervised by the Scientific Commitee

In order to start a project a researcher creates a document describing the subject or research, its minimal budget an minimal delay.
If the project is a sub-project of another project then it is submitted for approval to the project leader of the parent project, else it is submitted for approval to the Scientific Commitee.

Once accepted, the project lead can receive funds.
The funds, registered on the project account, are spent by the project lead.
Each expense is associated to an invoice that is accepted and paid by Manaty.
An expense can only be made if its amount is lower than the project account balance.
Any member of the Scientific Comitee can see the list of expenses.

The project lead commits to organize recorded video webinars every month.
The total amount of time of those monthly video must be at least the amount financed by the Scientific Commitee.
The project lead commits to answer monthly the questions sent by the scientific commitee.
The scientific comitee can decide at any moment to stop a project and reimburse the remaining funds.
In case the project lead do not fulfill its obligations.

Scientific commitee

The scientific commitee is composed of all individuals and organizations that finance the research projects.
It decides with a simple majority of expressed composed votes :

  • [D1] Whether an individual is accepted as a researcher.
  • [D2] Whether a project (not attached to another project) is accepted.
  • [D3] If a project need to be stopped.

Any individual or company that finance a research project is allowed to join the scientific project.
He can vote directly any decision or delegate his vote to another member (provided this later accepts), potentially for a limited time and limited scope (D1, D2 or D3).

To finance a project, the individual send request on discord.
He will then receive a proposal from Manaty, and if accepted, an invoice.
The service sold correspond to a certain amount of minutes of video webinar to be performed by the project lead.
The current rate is 100eur per minute.
